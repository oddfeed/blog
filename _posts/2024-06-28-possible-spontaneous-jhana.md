# Spontaneous Jhana Entry

I believe I’ve slipped into the first jhana spontaneously whilst going through my daily life, I’ve felt all tension release from my body and pain become the same as pleasure, my mind is clear and focused at the same time, my body feels light and I cannot tell where it ends, all I feel is bliss. 

It’s dwindling now, I cannot be certain if this is/was a jhana state or if it’s something else.
