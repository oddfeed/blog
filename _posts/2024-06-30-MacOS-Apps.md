# MacOS Apps
- **[Bartender 5](https://www.macbartender.com/Bartender5/)**, An organiser with lots of features for the MacOS menu bar
- **[Alfred](https://www.alfredapp.com/)**, A replacement for spotlight with lots of useful features such as searching websites, your calendar, making calendar events etc etc
- **[Setapp](https://setapp.com/)**, A single subscription service that gives you access to a lot of different apps, if you use a lot of the apps included it can save money.
	- **[Hazeover](https://hazeover.com/)**, Dims background windows to make the main window pop out more
	- **[Hustl](https://gohustl.co/)**, Makes a Timelapse of your screen, useful for showing off/tracking work in a visual manner 
	- **[Dato](https://apps.apple.com/us/app/dato/id1470584107)**, A small menubar calendar plugin, can also show the next calendar event in your menubar
	- **[Hand Mirror](https://handmirror.app)**, check your webcam/mic before joining meetings quickly and easily
	- **[SideNotes](https://www.apptorium.com/sidenotes)**, lightweight notes app meant to be used as a sort of scratchpad, syncs to IOS
	- **[Ulysses](https://ulysses.app/)**, Comprehensive writing software with lots of features
	- **[CleanShot X](https://cleanshot.com/)**, Screenshot/screenrecord tool with lots of very useful features such as highlighting text, blurring text etc
	- **[Daily](https://dailytimetracking.com/)**, Simple time tracker that just pops up every now and then asking what youre doing, has useful export options for sending to clients
- **[Adguard for Safari](https://adguard.com/en/adguard-safari/overview.html)**, solid ad blocker for safari
- **[Dropover](https://dropoverapp.com/)**, when you drag something a tray pops up letting you keep multiple items there nice and easy, also lets you share via a cloud link (iCloud, dropbox etc)
- **[Super Agent](https://super-agent.com/)**, setup your preferences then it auto fills out cookie banners saving you time and effort
- **[Little Snitch Mini](https://www.obdev.at/products/littlesnitch-mini/index.html)**, shows you what applications are using your network and lets you block them, if it doesn’t need the internet don’t let it use the internet
- **[Klib](http://klib.me/)**, export highlights from Apple Books or kindle etc
- **[Middle](https://middleclick.app/)**, three finger click now = middle mouse click on MacBook
- **[Mic Drop](https://getmicdrop.com/)**, mute your mic from the menu bar
- **[Better Dictation](https://betterdictation.com/)**, press command+v and talk that then gets transcribed for you (locally, no data gets sent) with high accuracy and speed, very easy for long emails and more private than apples feature
