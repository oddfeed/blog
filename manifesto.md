Introduction
============

The early memories I have of technology are those of control and expansion, my dad using aquatic equipment to watercool a custom build, cracking drm on software, teaching me how to use tools to crack drm on blockbuster rentals, how to load/play cracked PS2 games, it was all about control - The philosophy was simple, I own my computer and I can modify it as I see fit, your right for intellectual property does not supersede my ownership of my machine and part of that ownership includes being able to run any combination of 1s and 0s I want.
